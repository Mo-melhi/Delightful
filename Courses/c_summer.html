Industrial Engineering

Multi-Criteria Decision Making

This course deliberates decision-making under uncertainty by addressing Multi-Criteria Decision Making (MCDM) techniques. Learn to evaluate alternatives using multiple conflicting objectives with methods including AHP, ANP, and TOPSIS.
