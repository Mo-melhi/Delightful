Application Requirements

01

Student Status

You must be an undergraduate or postgraduate student currently enrolled in a university.

02

English Proficiency

An appropriate English level is required (B2 level is recommended) to fully participate in the program.

03

Open to All Fields

Students from any field of study are welcome - medicine, engineering, social sciences, and more!

How to Apply?

1

Apply Online

Complete the application form and upload:

  • Passport (photo ID page)
  • University ID (or approval letter)
  • Passport-size photo

Apply Now ‚Üí

2

Pay and Get Accepted

Once your application is received, you will get a preliminary acceptance with payment instructions.

  • You have one week to complete the payment (online or bank transfer).
  • After you send the Payment Receipt, you will receive your Official Acceptance Letter.

3

Submit Final Forms

At least 2 weeks before the program starts, you must submit two Google Forms:

  • Travel Information (including arrival/departure details and travel insurance)
  • To-Do List (for selecting optional tours)
